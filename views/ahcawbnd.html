Wool Insights

In 2021-22, the total wool production in India was 33.13 thousand tonnes, which was a 10.30% decrease from the previous year. India's annual wool production is around 43-46 million kg, which is about 2% of the world's total production. India's wool and woolen industry is the seventh-largest in the world and is an important sector of India's rural economy. About 85% of India's wool production is carpet grade wool, 5% is apparel grade, and 10% is coarser grade wool for making blankets.
